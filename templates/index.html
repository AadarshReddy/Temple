About Temple

Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple, popularly known as the Tirumala Balaji Temple, is one of the most revered and visited pilgrimage sites in the world. Located in the Tirumala Hills of Andhra Pradesh, it is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, believed to have appeared in this sacred place to save mankind from the trials and troubles of the Kali Yuga.
